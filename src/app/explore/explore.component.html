Good to Know

Weather

September is the rainy season — warm (22–27°C) with afternoon showers. Bring a light layer.

Getting Around

Uber and InDrive work well. The Metro is great for sightseeing. Avoid taxis off the street.

Currency

Colombian Peso (COP). ATMs are widely available. Cards accepted at most restaurants.
